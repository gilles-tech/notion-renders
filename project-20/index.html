⊙ READING THE FLOWCHART · WHAT TO FIX FIRST

Three priority bands. Six screens are P0.

Out of 12 screens, 6 are P0 — bugs there destroy trust faster than the rest of the experience can recover it. P1 is the layer of features users explicitly missed. P2 is the polish that turns a working product into a product they recommend.

P0 · STOP THE BLEEDING

6 screens · 23 critical bugs

  • Step 02 · Auth — fix LinkedIn blank-page bug, use the data once collected.
  • Step 05 · Loading — replace \"Hello Gilles\" + skeletons with narrated 5-step process.
  • Step 06 · Dashboard — make \"non défini\" fields editable; reframe % as completion; lift Mission Card to top.
  • Step 09 · Target — confidence ≥ 80%; no more army general / infirmier / secrétaire de mairie.
  • Step 10 · Offers — fix stale-state bug; add filters; integrate APEC; show match scores.
  • Step 11 · CV Studio — fix PDF generation; offer 4 templates (no black background); preserve original language.
P1 · MAKE IT GUIDE

What users explicitly missed

  • Actionable scoring — every score is a CTA, every gap is a door (Step 08). Scores without paths to fix are observations, not tools.
  • Sector-aware advice — replace IT-tech vocabulary with marketing / finance / HR / commerce contextualization (Step 08 + Step 11).
  • Edit pictograms everywhere — users can't guess fields are editable (Step 07). Every editable field gets a pencil icon.
  • First-time onboarding tour — contextual tooltips on each module's first visit (Step 12).
  • Use LinkedIn data — pre-populate profile from OAuth (Step 02 → Step 07). Currently collected and ignored.
P2 · FROM WORKING TO RECOMMENDED

The polish that compounds

  • Surprise discovery — surface adjacent roles where skills transfer (Florence + Emmanuel both asked).
  • Calendar integration — \"take 2h Tuesday to refresh your CV\" (Emmanuel).
  • Coach Connect option — premium tier with human accompaniment (Emmanuel: \"that's different, that's interesting\").
  • I18n cleanup — proper FR/EN toggle in topbar; never mix within an entry; consistent vouvoiement for senior B2B.
  • Editorial polish — fix orthographic mistakes; replace radar charts with sortable lists; tighten CV design QA.
  • One-shot pricing — Florence: \"students would pay 9.99€ for a single month to redo their CV.\" Add à-la-carte alongside the subscription.
